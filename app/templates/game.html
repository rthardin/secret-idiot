    How to Play

    One player is secretly the Agent. Their mission: complete a covert task in public without the Crowd catching on — while making sure the Witness sees it happen.

    Agent

    You have a secret mission. Complete it visibly enough that your Witness can confirm it — but discreetly enough that the Crowd doesn't notice.

    Witness

    You know who the Agent is and what their mission is. Observe quietly. Don't tip off the Crowd, and don't hover — the Agent will come to you.

    Crowd

    You have no idea who the Agent is or what they're doing. Watch everyone for suspicious behaviour. You can accuse (burn) someone in the debrief.
